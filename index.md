## Lesson overview

- **Audience:** Astronomers using (or planning to use) ML/AI in research  
- **Level:** Introductory / cross‑career  
- **Estimated time:** ~45 minutes total  
- **Format:** Discussion + short activities (no live coding)

**Learning objectives:**
- Understand what reproducibility means *specifically* for ML in astronomy
- Identify common reproducibility failure modes in ML workflows
- Recognise ethical risks specific to ML/AI in astronomy research
- Apply practical, lightweight practices to improve reproducibility and ethical use
